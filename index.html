Welcome

We are interested in your shopping habits. Specifically, a large retail chain that is planning to open a food store in your area wants to know consumers' food preferences.

Imagine you are in a grocery store now! You will see several food items that are available in the food isle. Your task is simple; you just have to indicate whether you would buy this product if you were shopping now. You will see about 20 food items, one at a time. If you want to buy the item, just click on "ADD TO CART" button. If you do not want to buy the item, click on "CONTINUE SHOPPING" button.
